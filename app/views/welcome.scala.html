My name is Jacob Enget, and I am a software engineer that has worked at both id Software and Havok. Because the projects I worked on daily in the video game industry weren't something I could demonstrate or talk about publicly I created this website to present personal projects to the world and express a bit more about myself.
Most of the projects you find on this site are many years old and were motivated by classes I took when studying computer science at the University of Minnesota. The exception is the Doom Asset Viewer that I wrote this year (2015) to gain practice in build something substantial in Scala and to dig into the details of the WAD file format.
My first academic love was math, which explains a bit of my nerd slant. But a year and a half after finishing my B.S. in mathematics I realized that I wanted a career doing something more social and emotionally creative. This, combined with my long love affair with animation and video games, led me back to school to study computer science. Since then I've been happily working with computers and math together.
Besides the demonstrations on this site, you'll also find my resume and information on how to contact me. I hope you enjoy looking around, and it would be great to hear from you!
